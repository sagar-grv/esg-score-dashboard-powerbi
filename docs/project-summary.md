# Project Summary

## Problem

ESG information collected from different community groups is difficult to compare when it is distributed across separate records or reports.

## Solution

This Power BI dashboard organises Environmental, Social, and Governance indicators into stakeholder-specific pages and a consolidated Shirpur view. It enables users to compare ESG dimensions and observe the overall risk score in a clear visual format.

## Intended Users

- Researchers
- Educational institutions
- Local development organisations
- ESG and sustainability teams
- Community-programme coordinators

## Main Metrics

- Environmental Score
- Social Score
- Governance Score
- Overall Risk Score

## Portfolio Keywords

Power BI, ESG Analytics, Sustainability Analytics, Dashboard Development, KPI Analysis, Risk Analysis, Data Visualisation, Business Intelligence, Data Storytelling
