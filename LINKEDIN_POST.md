# LinkedIn Post Draft

I built an **ESG Score Analysis Dashboard** in Microsoft Power BI to turn a complex dataset into a clear analytical journey.

### The problem

The information spans multiple dimensions, and a static table makes it difficult to move from the overall picture to the exact segment that needs attention.

### The approach

This report follows a **segment-to-community** storyline:

- **Start with stakeholder voices** — dedicated pages isolate Health Workers, Homemakers, Students, Farmers and Teachers so each group can be reviewed without mixing populations.
- **Move to the Shirpur view** — the community-level page brings the three ESG dimensions together.
- **Close with risk context** — the final page places ESG scores beside the overall risk score, enabling a discussion of where sustainability performance may need attention.

The repository avoids claiming unsupported numerical conclusions because the original semantic model is remotely connected and the source data was not supplied separately.
